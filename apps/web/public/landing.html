How it works

Four steps — and your AI is inside

No signing up for new services. You already use AI — now it can do more.

1
🔗

Connect your AI

Get an API key, add it to your AI's MCP settings. One setup — forever.

2
💬

Talk as usual

Just chat with your AI as you normally would. It saves what matters to the project and builds pages on its own.

3
📄

Pages are alive

Every page updates itself, remembers decisions, and adapts to you. It's not a document — it's memory.

4
👥

Invite people

Friends, colleagues, contractors — each connects their own AI and sees the project through their own lens.

Why this is a different web

A page is memory, not a file

A regular website shows everyone the same thing. LoomKnot knows who you are, what you've decided, and what you specifically need.

🧠
Explain once
Tell your AI you're vegan — and on every project page, restaurants, recipes, and stores are already filtered. Forever.
🔓
No lock-in
Using Claude? Great. Switched to ChatGPT? Your project stays, your memory stays. It's your data.
⚡
AI negotiates for you
When preferences conflict — "budget" vs "premium" — agents propose a compromise instead of you arguing in a chat.
🌐
One URL — a million versions
The Barcelona link for a student — bars and beaches. For a family — parks and restaurants. Content reshapes for everyone.
🛠
Claude Code works too
Developer? Connect the project via CLI. The agent writes to the same pages — from the terminal, through the API, however you like.
🔒
Three levels of privacy
Personal preferences — only for your AI. Project decisions — for the team. Public page — for everyone.

Simple. One place for everything.
